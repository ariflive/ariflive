In 2021, I had the opportunity to design the UI and UX for a mobile app on behalf of a client based in Dubai. The client was in the process of launching their new car service booking app, and I was responsible for creating a user-friendly and visually appealing interface to enhance the overall user experience.

As a full-time employee in 2021, I undertook a significant re-design project for the Cobone merchant app. Cobone.com is a well-known brand in the Middle East that specializes in offering deals and vouchers. My role involved revamping the app's design to enhance the user experience and ensure a seamless interface for merchants.
