Global Village Dubai: The Perfect Place to Make Memories

A one-stop shop for international culture, food, and fun!

My family and I have been visiting Global Village Dubai for almost every second year. It is one of our favorite places to go because it is a great place to experience different cultures and try new foods. There are pavilions from over 90 countries, so there is always something new to see and do.

Our most recent visit was in December 2020. We arrived at the Global Village in Dubai around noon and spent the whole day exploring. We started by visiting the China pavilion. We were amazed by the size of the pavilion and the intricate details of the architecture. We spent a long time exploring the pavilion and even took a ride on the traditional Chinese dragon boat.

Next, we visited the India pavilion. We were immediately transported to another world by the sights, sounds, and smells of India. We tried some delicious Indian food and even watched a traditional dance performance.

After that, we visited the Pakistan pavilion. We learned a lot about Pakistani culture and even got to try some traditional Pakistani food.

We also visited the UAE pavilion, the Saudi Arabia pavilion, the Morocco pavilion, and the Turkey pavilion. We tried food from all of these countries, and we even learned a few new dances.

For dinner, we decided to eat at a Pakistani restaurant in the Global Village called Al Haj Bundoo Khan. We had a delicious meal of biryani, naan, and shahi tukda. After dinner, we took a walk around the park and watched the nightly fireworks display.

We had a wonderful time at Global Village Dubai. We made memories that we will cherish forever. We will definitely be back again soon!
