10-Year Anniversary at Middle East Digital Group Dubai

I am grateful for the opportunity to have worked at Middle East Digital Group in Dubai for the past 10 years.

On November 1, 2021, I was working in my office when my colleagues suddenly came together and called me into the meeting hall. I went there, and everyone was ready to congratulate me on completing my 10 years at Middle East Digital Group Dubai. My company CEO, Ms. Jane, was also there.

She talked about my service length in the company and how proud she was of my accomplishments. I was very happy to hear this, and I was honored that she would take the time to congratulate me personally.

Ms. Jane then presented me with a gift. I was so surprised and grateful, and I thanked her profusely.

After that, we all gathered in the meeting hall for cake and refreshments. We talked about our favorite memories from the past 10 years, and we laughed and joked. It was a truly special day.

I was so humbled by the outpouring of support from my colleagues and my CEO. I knew that I was lucky to be a part of such a wonderful team, and I was grateful for the opportunity to have worked at Middle East Digital Group Dubai for the past 10 years.

That day will remain a historical day for me for the rest of my life. It was a day when I felt truly appreciated and valued, and it was a day that I will never forget.
